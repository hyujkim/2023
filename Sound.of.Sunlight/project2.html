08/2021The project is about converting light data into sound. Inspired by the color and brightness of sunlight, I wanted to reflect the slight difference between the brighter side and shadow.

I used an electronic oscillator instead of a pre-recorded sound file to make the process like the raw data of light naturally generates specific sound. The design using a mirror led to indirect connection between the sun and the sensors by facing them to the mirror. As a result, users can get much easier interaction that can control the sound just by tilting the device.
