12/2021Inspired from Louis Borges’s short story, “Tlon, Uqbar, Orbis Tertius”. In this story, there is an old local area called “Tlon” that only exist in an ancient document. In this “Tlon” only lost and forgotten things appear and start living. I interpreted this story as a tale of two parallel worlds, one is the one we are living with, the other is the world of perception. This concept is similar to today’s culture in that people are naturally living with the virtual world alongside.

The word “decaying” is only applied to the physical world. This is ironic when people normally say authentic belongs to this real world. I wanted to flip this idea by experimenting melt, crush or evaporating the materials.
