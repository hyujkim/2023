08/2022It is a combination of two signals by converting light signals into a soundwave. My task is to design these signals into something else. As for light, substitute amplitude as brightness and frequencies as color. As for sound, the volume is amplitude, and pitch is frequency. Following these factors, you can enter brightness to modulate the volume and RGB data to control pitch. In that way, two different signals communicate with the very same wave.
