12/2019This project was about making a visual approach to the concept of 'time'. I brought the form of solarium to illustrate this concept. SharingʽTime΄ is not just about delivering numerical information. Sense of existence in certain times and spaces is more of a physical property than figures and numbers. It is different from manipulating clocks.

It connects with experiencing the temperature of moving sunlight and noticing that shadows became more blurry than an hour ago. How can we make the sunlight΄ we know as small as to hold into our hands? If this smaller ʽsunlight΄ moves identical with each side, could we say that we share our time?
