08/2021Tried to make a score composed of pencil drawing. Due to the graphite’s conductivity, so that Arduino can detect the different amounts of voltage in each density of graphite drawing.I already measured of frequencies of each density can make, intend to compose it as a score.

The result will be made as a form of a stethoscope for drawing, the participant can hear the sound interpreted from the drawing elements. The score is designed by drawings, that can be directly interpreted into a musical note.
